FAQ

Frequently asked questions about design

1. How can I add my logo on a sweatshirt?
To add a logo to your sweatshirt, you simply need to upload the photo or logo in one of the supported file types. Possible file formats are JPG, GIF, PNG, or BMP. Make sure the design is properly isolated, meaning there’s no background in it (including any color backgrounds). Then put the logo where you want to have in your sweatshirt, resize it if necessary – and you are good to go.

Designing a custom t-shirt with Einkc is easy. Start by picking the type of t-shirt you want to print on, your personalized t-shirt design, and the placement of your design.
