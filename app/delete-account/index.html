How Do I Delete My Account on Multiple Custom?

Last updated on Nov.2022


* Note :

- All your data will be deleted and can not be recovered.

- You cannot review in-app notifications after your account is deleted.

- You can register new account again by using your current social network account.

To delete your account, please log in to Money Tracker mobile app > Go to Account page > Select Account Management > Click the Delete Account button at the bottom.
